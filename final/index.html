Newbury Street

This is a map we designed for Newbury Street. Newbury Street is Boston's most enchanting street. Eight blocks filled with salons, boutiques, and fabulous dining. Boston's Newbury Street has something for everyone. So in our map, we divided all the destinations to four categories, which are treat Yo self (luxuries shopping), Average shopping, Let’s get creative (gallery art day), Self Care and Relaxation. So no matter what is your goal, you will find a path belongs to you!
